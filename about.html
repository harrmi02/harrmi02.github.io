Who am I?

Hello! I’m Miriam Harries, an enthusiastic student of Computer Science, Statistics, and Theater. I’m originally from Bloomington, Minnesota, and right now I’m mid-way through my final year of undergraduate studies at Luther College in the beautiful town of Decorah, Iowa. When I’m not writing code, I spend my time working on theater productions with Luther’s Department of Visual and Performing Arts and acting as the technical director for the student-led SPIN Theater. My favorite projects are those that make an emotional connection with the audience or user, whether they do it by being profound or being fun. If it makes you smile, it’s worth doing.
After graduation this spring, I plan to attend graduate school to learn how I can combine my technical skills with my love of theater and the arts. After that, the future is wide open…but I am aiming towards a career in Disney Imagineering or story-focused game development.
